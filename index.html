Why Switch From Traditional IT to Cloud Computing?

Improper Scalability

This includes the installation and upgrades of the hardware in the specialized personnel. This process can cost a company around $6,000, where the price can rise depending on the complexity of the company's server infrastructure.

Slow Deployment and Management

This is where organizations are said to experience a surplus of available computing power. About 10-30 percent of computing power is actually used from a company's data center server. And only less than 5 percent of employees take the advantage of that availability.

High Maintaince Cost of Infrastructure

This is where the deployment of traditional IT, reduces a company's entry. thus, also the competitive advantage. This is because traditional IT requires the construction of a space, specifically designed to maintain the IT system secure.
